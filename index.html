What is ax? Ax is a Caas (Content-as-a-Service) software
that turns your data into stories.

The software is based on a highly sophisticated algorithm that identifies and interprets your data and
turns it into meaningful, personalized information. Available in up to 13 languages, AX is highly flexible
and scalable, and can be used in various areas, from news to sports to weather, to financial reports
and product information.
